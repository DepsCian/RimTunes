## Two Ways to Play

Choose a mode on first launch and change it anytime in Settings.

- **Dynamic Music System** — RimTunes selects music to fit your colony's current situation.
- **Manual Mode** — Full control over playlists, song order, shuffle, repeat, and playback.

> **Both modes include:** custom music import, playlists, song tags, hotkeys, volume normalization, smooth transitions, and the mini-player overlay.

## Dynamic Music System

Turn your library into a living RimWorld soundtrack. Tag songs for specific conditions and let RimTunes select suitable tracks automatically.

- **Danger** — Tense music during raids and combat, calmer music when danger passes
- **Time of day** — Daytime, nighttime, and custom time ranges
- **Weather** — Rain, snow, fog, toxic fallout, and more
- **Seasons** — Spring, summer, fall, and winter
- **Game conditions** — Map-wide events and conditions, including supported mod content
- **Biomes** — Deserts, forests, ice sheets, tropical maps, and more
- **Royal titles** — Optional Royalty DLC integration

RimTunes avoids recently played tracks, supports smooth transitions, and can use either your whole library or one selected playlist.

> **Example:** Tag a song as **Peaceful + Night + Winter** for quiet snowy evenings, then forbid it during danger so it never plays in a raid.

## Mini-Player Overlay

Control your music without opening the main window.

- Draggable overlay with corner snapping
- Play, pause, next, and previous controls
- Seek bar with click-and-drag seeking
- Scrolling song titles for long names
- Double-click to open the main RimTunes window
- Auto-hiding controls and lockable position
- Automatically avoids RimWorld tutorial popups
- Scales with RimWorld's interface scale
- Customizable background, text, button, and progress-bar colors
- Silence countdown between tracks

## Import Your Own Music

- **Local files** — Import music from your computer
- **Direct audio links** — Add supported audio files from direct URLs
- **Existing game music** — Browse RimWorld music and music included with installed mods

When available, RimTunes automatically reads artist and title information from audio files.

### Supported Formats

- **`.mp3`**
- **`.wav`**
- **`.ogg`**

> Tracker formats such as **`.xm`**, **`.it`**, **`.mod`**, and **`.s3m`** are not supported.

## Playlists

Create playlists for every colony, biome, mood, faction, or disaster.

- Create, rename, delete, and reorder playlists
- Drag and drop songs to change their order
- Copy and paste songs between playlists
- Merge playlists without duplicate tracks
- Choose custom playlist icons
- Shuffle tracks and choose repeat modes
- Export and import playlists through the clipboard
- **Library** — Browse every available song in one place
- **Mod playlists** — Automatically created for installed mods that include music

## Song Tags

Tell the Dynamic Music System when a song should, or should not, play.

| Category | Available tags |
| --- | --- |
| Mood | Tense, Peaceful, Ambient |
| Time of day | Day, Night, and custom time ranges |
| Seasons | Spring, Summer, Fall, Winter |
| Environment | Weather conditions and biomes |
| Events | Game conditions and events |
| Royalty | Royal titles, when the DLC is installed |

Each tag can be set separately for every song:

- **Default** — No preference
- **Required** — The condition must be active
- **Forbidden** — The song will not play while the condition is active

## Playback

- Smooth crossfades between songs
- Separate transition settings for peaceful and dangerous situations
- Optional silence gaps between tracks
- Shorter breaks during combat and longer breaks during peaceful gameplay
- Optional idle silence after a period without activity
- Pause or skip silence gaps whenever you want
- Optional volume normalization for more consistent music volume

## Custom Hotkeys

Five fully rebindable hotkeys:

- Open or close the main music window
- Show or hide the mini-player overlay
- Pause or resume playback
- Next song
- Previous song

Supports **Ctrl**, **Alt**, **Shift**, and **Cmd** modifiers.

## Mod Compatibility

- Automatically discovers music included with installed mods
- Creates separate playlists for mod soundtracks
- Keeps RimWorld's music volume synchronized with RimTunes
- Works with vanilla music and supported mod-added music
